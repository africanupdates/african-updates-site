title: "Umuryango PFR Inkotanyi ugiye gu"
draft: true
---

Umuryango FPR Inkotanyi watangaje ko guhera tariki 24 Gashyantare 2024, hazatangira amatora azahera ku rwego rw’Umudugudu yo guhitamo abazahagararira uyu Muryango mu matora rusange ya Perezida wa Repubulika n’ay’Abadepite ateganyijwe muri Nyakanga 2024.

Ibyo biri mu itangazo Umuryango FPR Inkotanyi wasohoye mu gitondo cyo kuri uyu wa gatanu tariki ya 23 gashyantare 2024 binyuze ku rubuga rwa X yahoze ari Twitter.

iri tangazo rivuga ko aya matora azahera ku rwego rw’umudugudu asorezwe ku rwego rw’igihugu mu nama nkuru y’umuryango iteganijwe muri werurwe.
