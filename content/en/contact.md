## Reach the Team

Whether you have a news tip, feedback on our coverage, a correction, or a general enquiry, we're glad to hear from you. Use whichever method is easiest for you.

### Email

For all enquiries, story tips, and feedback, email us directly:

**[info@africanupdates.com](mailto:info@africanupdates.com)**

We read every message and aim to respond as quickly as we can.

### WhatsApp Channel

Follow our WhatsApp Channel for the latest headlines delivered straight to your phone:

**[Join our WhatsApp Channel](https://whatsapp.com/channel/0029VafqKm311ulSPJHML00o)**

### Social Media

Stay connected and join the conversation:

Follow us on [X (Twitter)](https://x.com/africanupdates1) for breaking news and updates.

## Send Us a News Tip

Have a story we should know about? Email us at [info@africanupdates.com](mailto:info@africanupdates.com?subject=News%20Tip) with as much detail as you can. If you'd like to remain anonymous, let us know in your message and we'll respect your request.

## Corrections

Accuracy matters to us. If you spot an error in one of our stories, please email [info@africanupdates.com](mailto:info@africanupdates.com?subject=Correction) with the article title and the details, and we'll review it promptly.
